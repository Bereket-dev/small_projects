FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What makes your coffee special?

    ❱
    Our coffee is crafted with passion, using only the highest-quality, hand-selected beans. We source from the best coffee-growing regions, ensuring a rich and smooth flavor. Each batch is roasted to perfection, bringing out unique tasting notes that make every cup exceptional.

  • Do you offer decaf options?

    ❱
    Yes! We offer a selection of decaffeinated coffee made using a natural, chemical-free process that preserves the rich flavors while removing caffeine. Whether you prefer whole beans, ground coffee, or single-serve pods, we have a decaf option for you.

  • How do I choose the right coffee for me?

    ❱
    Choosing the right coffee depends on your taste preferences. If you enjoy a bold and intense flavor, go for a dark roast. If you like a balanced, smooth cup, a medium roast is ideal. For a bright and fruity taste, try a light roast. Need help? Our team is happy to guide you!

  • Is your coffee ethically sourced?

    ❱
    Absolutely! We are committed to ethical sourcing and work directly with farmers to ensure fair wages and sustainable farming practices. Our beans are certified by reputable organizations such as Fair Trade, Rainforest Alliance, and Organic certifications.

  •  How soon will my order arrive?

    ❱
    Orders are typically processed within 24 hours and shipped via standard or expedited delivery. Depending on your location, you can expect your coffee to arrive within 3-5 business days for standard shipping or 1-2 business days for express options.
